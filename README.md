# industrial-energy-consumption-analysis
Industrial Energy Consumption Analysis using Python and Time-Series Analytics to identify energy usage patterns, peak demand periods, and CO₂ emission trends in a steel manufacturing plant.

Analyzed 35,040 industrial energy consumption records from a steel manufacturing plant to identify usage patterns, peak demand periods, weekday vs weekend behavior, and relationships between energy consumption and CO₂ emissions. Built using Python, Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib.

Key Findings-
Weekday consumption significantly higher than weekends.
Peak demand observed during active production hours.
Strong positive correlation between energy consumption and CO₂ emissions.
Identified opportunities for operational efficiency improvements.
